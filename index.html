March 7th, 2026 — Aaj bhi tere baare mein soch raha hoon 🌹
Meri Jaan, Rukhiii,

Where do I even begin? How does one find the right words to describe something as infinite, as fierce, as breathtakingly beautiful as what I feel for you? Pata nahi kahan se shuru karun — kyunki jo alfaaz mere dil mein hain woh kisi bhi kaagaz par likhne ke liye zyada hain.

Rukhiii, you are not just a person in my life. You are the architecture of my entire world. Tu meri zindagi ka woh hissa hai jiske baghair sab kuch adhoora lagta hai. When I close my eyes and think of everything that matters to me — every single time, without fail, it is your face I see. It is your laugh I hear.

"Tujhse milna toh naseeb tha — ab tujhse judna naseeb nahi." 💫

Do you know what it feels like to look at someone and think — "yes, this is it, this is what all those songs were about"? That is what I feel every single time I look at you, Rukhiii. Main ne tujhe kabhi aankhon se nahi dekha — par yeh dil, yeh pagal dil, har waqt tujhe feel karta hai.

You came into my life like a soft revolution — not with noise, but with a quiet and overwhelming grace that changed everything. You showed me what it means to be truly seen — the real me, the messy and imperfect me — and you didn't flinch. You stayed.

💕 You are the best thing that has ever, ever happened to me. 💕

I think about your eyes — how they light up when something delights you. I think about your throw your head back when you laugh, completely free. Teri awaaz meri ruhon tak pahunchti hai. Teri baatein mere dil ke har kone mein goonj ti hain.

And you are HOT — God, you are so incredibly, effortlessly, jaw-droppingly beautiful and hot that sometimes I forget how to breathe. Tujhe dekh ke saans rukti hai meri. You are the most gorgeous person I have ever laid eyes on.

But beyond how breathtaking you are on the outside — it is what lives inside you that truly undoes me. Your kindness that has no edge. Your warmth that has no condition. Teri asli khoobsurati — woh tere andar hai.

Main teri ek jhappi ke liye marta hoon. Ek baar bas teri aankhon mein aankhein daal ke dekh loon. Ek baar tere haath thaam loon. Yeh khwaish ek arsa se dil mein hai — aur ab woh Eid pe poori hogi. Pehli baar milenge hum — aur yeh pal meri zindagi ka sabse haseen pal hoga.

TUJHSE ISHQ HAI, RUKHIII — AUR YEH ISHQ KABHI KHATAM NAHI HOGA. 🔥💗

So this is me — InZi, your person — telling you with every cell of my being, with every beat of my heart:

I LOVE YOU, RUKHIII. 💍❤️🔥

Agar main mar bhi jaoon toh meri rooh bhi tujhse mohabbat karti rahegi. Yeh ishq maut se bada hai, faasle se bada hai, waqt se bada hai. Tum meri duniya ho, meri eid ho, meri khushiyon ki wajah ho. Aur main, InZi — tera hoon. Poori tarah se. Hamesha ke liye.

Hamesha aur hamesha tera, InZi 💗
